Accomplishments

  • Development of the Hero Grid Component on WCP

    As the lead developer, I spearheaded the implementation of the new Hero Grid Component for JPL’s internal WCP platform. This component enhances WCP by providing an advanced hero interface that empowers site owners to effectively disseminate pages, documents, media, and other content via the presentation of 2-5 Hero Cards. My responsibilities encompassed collaborative efforts with team members and internal stakeholders, feature development, testing and quality assurance protocols, and deployment.

    I take pride in successfully delivering the development work for this project within stringent time and budgetary constraints. The stakeholder expressed considerable satisfaction upon reviewing and using the Hero Grid component.

    I was awarded a JPL Voyager Award for my contributions to this project.

  • Ed Stone Trail Webpage

    As the primary web producer, I developed the Ed Stone Trail webpage, which was launched in conjunction with the laboratory-wide event commemorating Dr. Ed Stone. In collaboration with management and the DesignLab Graphics team, I facilitated the communication of Dr. Ed Stone's narrative through key Voyager milestones.

    This project provided an excellent opportunity to engage in cross-functional collaboration with the DesignLab in support of a task assigned by Laboratory Leadership. The undertaking presented several challenges, including a tight deadline and evolving content specifications, primarily due to constraints within the content management system. Despite these obstacles, I successfully managed the project to completion, ensuring its availability for the event and subsequent Laboratory-wide communications.

    At JPL, I've spent the last few years working on a variety of projects. These include core CreativeStudio initiatives, support for NASA's web modernization, and new development opportunities, among others.
